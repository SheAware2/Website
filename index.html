About SheAware

She Aware is a nonprofit organization dedicated to empowering school dropouts, young women in slums, and girls from marginalized rural villages with essential life and technology skills. Across Uganda, millions of girls face overwhelming barriers—digital exclusion, poverty, and a lack of role models—often leaving them with little hope for their future.

We bridge this gap by bringing technology education and mentorship directly to these communities. Through our outreach programs, we work closely with local elders and leaders to create awareness, showing girls that through digital skills, they can earn a living, build a bright future, and never be left behind.

Our Mission

To bridge the digital divide by equipping underserved youth, school dropouts, and marginalized communities with technology, training, and innovative solutions that unlock economic opportunities and improve livelihoods.

Our Vision

A Future Where Every Girl Thrives in Tech and leadership, enabling them to confidently navigate the digital landscape and break cycles of poverty.

The Stark Reality

Digital Divide

Globally, fewer than 20% of girls have access to digital education. Lack of tech skills limits future employment.

Social Exclusion

Lack of digital literacy perpetuates cycles of poverty and social exclusion for millions of young women.

Forgotten Communities

In Ugandan villages and urban slums, a vast majority of girls drop out of school and lack access to STEM education, hindering national development.

Actualizing Our Vision

We don't just teach technology; we ignite futures. Our three-pillar approach ensures every girl becomes a confident digital citizen.

Digital Literacy

Equipping girls with foundational tech skills, from computer operations to coding, ensuring they aren't left behind in the digital age.

Mentorship

Connecting underprivileged girls with inspiring women leaders in tech to build confidence and map out ambitious career paths.

Community Outreach

Collaborating with local elders and leaders in slums and villages to create awareness and open doors for girls to dream again.

Our Hybrid Learning Model

Combining online modules with practical in-person workshops, tailored to meet local community needs.

01. Learn

Complete engaging online modules tailored to meet local community needs and access constraints.

02. Practice

Hands-on in-person workshops covering coding, AI basics, and digital entrepreneurship.

03. Lead

Mentoring peers and connecting with inspiring women leaders in STEM fields for real-world insights.

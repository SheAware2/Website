About She Aware

She Aware is a nonprofit organization dedicated to empowering underprivileged girls with essential life and technology skills. Across many African communities, girls face barriers to education, digital literacy and economic participation. The digital gender divide continues to grow, limiting opportunities for millions of young women. She Aware bridges this gap by providing technology education, mentorship and resources that help girls build confidence, gain digital skills and become leaders within their communities.
